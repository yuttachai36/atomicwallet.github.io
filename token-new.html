AWC Tokens will serve as an internal currency for Atomic Wallet users. As Atomic grows fast, the tokens will be in great demand.

AWC will be used

- As a payment for: coins listing, dedicated support managers.
- Discount for in-app exchange or buy crypto services.
- Previleges for in-app trading desk.
- Affiliate + bounty rewards.
- Stacking rewards for holders.

Users can receive AWC tokens by participating in Airdrop, Affiliate program and Bounty campaigns.
